# Result

Conclusion: **DOES_NOT_SUPPORT**.

The 2022–2023 development evidence does not support positive monthly pricing of
BTC downside beta in the fixed mature Binance USD-M universe. Evaluation (2024) and
confirmation (2025) remain sealed.

## Main evidence

- 24 action months; 21–25 rankable targets per month.
- High-minus-low downside-beta next-month return: -4.2085% per month; three-month
  block-bootstrap 95% interval [-11.7936%, +3.6694%].
- Spearman rank IC: -0.130637; block interval [-0.238952, -0.017066]. Only 20.83%
  of months had a positive IC.
- The 0.25x high-downside-beta long proxy after 52 bps underlying round-trip cost
  and the 4% full-plan hurdle averaged -0.6252% per month; interval
  [-2.8256%, +1.6949%].
- 2022 and 2023 high-minus-low spreads were both negative (-6.1381% and -2.2788%).
- Uncontrolled standardized downside-beta slope was -0.2101% per month. With total
  beta, total volatility, momentum, MAX, and volume controls it was +0.3505%, but
  the positive-direction HAC p-value was 0.3954 and its interval crossed zero.
- The 45-day and 90-day diagnostics also had negative spread, IC, and long-proxy
  means. They cannot replace the fixed 60-day predictor.

High-downside-beta selection beat high-total-beta by only +0.4072% per month and its
interval crossed zero. It underperformed high-total-volatility by -1.5268% per month.
Thus the result supplies no incremental, economically usable signal beyond taking
generic market risk.

## Interpretation and scope

This does not refute the cited broad-spot downside-risk premium. The source research
includes many more coins, inactive assets, market capitalization, and a different
venue/data construction; its own evidence says volatility explains much of the
effect. The result rejects transfer to this fixed current-survivor, liquid perpetual
universe and this predeclared monthly predictor.

The negative ranking is not authorization to invert the rule. A low-downside-beta
strategy would be a new, post-result hypothesis closely overlapping the already weak
single-leg low-volatility evidence and is prohibited by this family stop. No strategy
handoff or product object was generated.
